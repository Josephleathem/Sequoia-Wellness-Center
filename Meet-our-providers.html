Sequoia Wellness Providers

Rita Kinstle, MA, LPCC-S

With over three decades of experience in the mental health field, Rita, is a seasoned therapist dedicated to helping individuals navigate the complexities of trauma, PTSD, addiction, emotional challenges, and personal growth. A strong advocate for evidence-based care, Rita is certified in EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing), Brain spotting, and DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy), Internal Family Systems Therapy, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and is highly skilled in trauma-informed care.

Rita specializes in guiding clients through healing journeys marked by resilience and self-discovery. Whether addressing depression, anxiety, mood disorders, personality disorders trauma, PTSD, addiction, codependency, or other challenges. Rita brings a compassionate, client-centered approach to every session, empowering individuals to achieve meaningful and lasting change.

Rita has extensive experience working as an Independent Contractor with the Department of Defense, providing individual therapy to active military members and their families and Gold Star Families. This work has fueled a deep passion for supporting veterans and their loved ones, a cornerstone of Rita's practice.

As a Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor with Supervisor designation, Rita also enjoys mentoring the next generation of clinicians, fostering their growth and ethical practice. When not working directly with clients, Rita is deeply committed to professional development and staying at the forefront of advancements in trauma, addiction and mental health care.

Cristina Khuth, MSW, LISW-S

I am an Ohio Licensed Independent Social Worker with a supervisor endorsement. I have over 15 years of experience in the social work profession. I earned my undergraduate degree in Psychology at Temple University (Philadelphia, PA) and my Master of Social Work at Widener University (Chester, PA). I began my focus in clinical counseling and psychotherapy 6 years ago, after many years of serving vulnerable women, children and families.

I enjoy working with adults and youth experiencing mental health and emotional disorders, substance abuse and addiction, and family and relationship problems. I have a passion for working with the trauma impacted population and I am well trained in trauma-informed care, EMDR, and cognitive-based therapies, including Dialectal Behavioral Therapy. I am also licensed in the state of Indiana as a clinical social worker with the ability to provide services to Indiana residents seeking telehealth therapy.
