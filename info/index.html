Services and Treatments

At Sequoia Wellness Center, we offer a comprehensive range of services and treatment approaches designed to meet your unique mental health needs. Whether you are seeking individual therapy or specialized treatments, our team is here to provide compassionate care.

Services Provided

  • Comprehensive Diagnostic Assessments
  • Consulting Services
  • Grief and Loss
  • Group Counseling
  • Individual Therapy
  • Marriage Therapy
  • Screening and testing for Depression Anxiety ADHD and PTSD
  • Substance Abuse Assessment
  • Telehealth

  • Individualized Treatment Approach's for Veterans and First Responders

Services Coming Soon

  • Peer Support

Treatment Approach's

  • Behavioral Modification
  • Brain Spotting Therapy
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT)
  • Experiential Therapy
  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)
  • Gestalt Therapy
  • Humanistic Therapy
  • Hypnotherapy
  • Internal Family Systems Therapy (IFS)
  • Motivational Interviewing in Addition Counseling

At Sequoia Wellness we are committed to making our services accessible to everyone by offering a flexible sliding scale for those who are underinsured or without insurance. For more details about how we can support you, please don't hesitate to contact our office.
